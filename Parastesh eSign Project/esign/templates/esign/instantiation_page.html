Welcome to the eSignature platform integrated with DocuSign.

By using this platform, you will be able to sign a predefined contract and send it to another signatory via email for them to sign.
In the end, you will both receive the signed document.

Follow these steps to sign a contract:
- Enter your name and email address as the first signatory.
- Enter the name and email address of the second signatory.
- Choose the contract you want to be signed.
